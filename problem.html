The $10 Billion Problem We're Solving

Organizations suffer from costly inefficiencies and mission failures due to silent sensor failures and degradation.

The Hidden Problem

Silent Sensor Degradation
Sensors fail without warning, providing incorrect data while appearing functional to system diagnostics[1]
Difficult-to-Diagnose Failures
75% of autonomous system failures are software-related[2], but sensor data quality issues often trigger these failures[3]
Altitude Misjudgments
Sensor malfunctions cause drones to make incorrect altitude judgments, leading to crashes and mission failures[4]
Preventable Downtime
20% of delay-causing maintenance actions could be avoided with proper sensor health monitoring[5]
Drone Operations
• 100x higher failure rate than manned aircraft[5]
• 50% of mishaps from component failures[5]
• 1 per 1,000 flight hours failure rate[5]
Autonomous Vehicles
• Disengagements from improper sensor readings
• Map and calibration data errors
• 75% of "software" failures are sensor-related
Financial Impact
• 2-5x higher unplanned maintenance costs[7]
• $5M+ major system replacement costs[7]
• 34% cost reduction possible with optimization[8]
