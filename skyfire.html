Technical Problems We Solve

📷 Visible Camera Problems

  • Motion blur from fixed exposure systems: Auto-exposure incompatible with movement photography
  • Poor low-light performance: Forces operations to avoid 90 minutes around sunrise/sunset
  • Inconsistent exposure during flight: Automatic adjustments create unusable image sequences
  • Limited dynamic range in high contrast scenes: Fixed aperture systems lose detail in shadows or highlights
  • Generic processing regardless of conditions: No adaptation to altitude, humidity, or atmospheric effects
  • Fixed noise reduction regardless of conditions: Same filtering applied without understanding actual noise sources
  • Binary quality assessment with no confidence metrics: No way to assess if imagery is suitable for analysis

🔥 LWIR/MWIR Camera Problems

  • No continuous calibration capability: Static factory calibration becomes invalid during operations
  • Platform thermal coupling interference: Heat from platform affects sensor accuracy with no compensation
  • Sensor self-heating drift: Progressive thermal drift during extended operations
  • Atmospheric interference compensation: No real-time correction for atmospheric attenuation effects
  • Environmental sensitivity: Performance varies dramatically with weather/altitude conditions
  • Temperature-dependent noise characteristics: Noise patterns change but processing doesn't adapt
  • Qualitative-only thermal imaging: Cannot provide precise temperature measurements for decision-making

Common Underlying Problem

Static, non-adaptive processing: All current systems use fixed algorithms that cannot respond to real-time physics changes in sensors or environment

Mission Impact on Law Enforcement Operations

📷 Visible Camera - Mission Effects

  • Motion blur → Mission failure during movement: Cannot capture usable imagery while advancing or during vehicle operations
  • Poor low-light → Operational window restrictions: Forces mission timing into narrow daylight windows, eliminating dawn/dusk tactical advantages
  • Inconsistent exposure → Intelligence gathering failure: Surveillance imagery unusable due to exposure variations, requiring mission repeats
  • Limited dynamic range → Target identification failure: Cannot simultaneously see into shadows and bright areas where threats may hide
  • Generic processing → Environmental mission limitations: Systems fail in varied conditions where law enforcement operates
  • Fixed noise reduction → False target identification: Processing removes real details or preserves noise as targets

🔥 LWIR/MWIR - Mission Effects

  • No continuous calibration → Progressive mission degradation: Thermal systems become unreliable during extended operations
  • Platform thermal coupling → False thermal signatures: Vehicle heat, electronics create false readings masking real threats
  • Sensor self-heating → Time-limited thermal capability: Thermal systems become increasingly inaccurate during long missions
  • Atmospheric interference → Range-limited thermal detection: Cannot reliably detect threats at operationally relevant distances
  • Environmental sensitivity → Geographic mission restrictions: Thermal systems unreliable in extreme environments
  • Qualitative-only thermal → Subjective threat assessment: Operators must make critical decisions based on interpretation rather than measurable data
